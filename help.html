Welcome to the help page.

1: To search for a particular movie just go to the search bar and type in the name of the movie.Or click the toggle button on the left of the home screen to reveal a side menu then select search.

2:To save a movie to your localStorage, click on the check out for the desired selection, after redirection click save to list.You will need an internet connection for the saved movie images to display.

3: To remove a movie from local storage , just click delete on the on the preffered selection.

4: To view a saved movie , just click check out on your preffered selection.
